About Me

Structured, Ethical & Evidence-Based Support

I am a Clinical Psychology postgraduate with formal training in counseling psychology, rehabilitation sciences, and therapeutic interventions. I hold an M.Sc in Clinical Psychology from CMR University, Bangalore, a Diploma in Counseling, and a Bachelor’s degree in Rehabilitation Science, Sociology, and Psychology.

My professional experience includes working in hospital settings, de-addiction centres, schools for children with special needs, and community mental health programs.

I believe psychological support should be structured, ethical, and grounded in evidence-based practices while remaining compassionate and respectful of each individual’s unique journey.
